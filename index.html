Frequently Asked Questions

          

            

              

                
Is my JSON data safe?

                
Yes. Formatting happens entirely in your browser. Your data is never sent to any server.

              

            

            

              

                
What is the file size limit?

                
Since processing is client-side, it depends on your browser's memory. Typically, files up to 10MB handle smoothly.

              

            

            

              

                
Can I use this offline?

                
Yes, the entire application works offline once loaded in your browser.
